GARMENTS

Denim is a heavy-weight fabric with very little drape or stretch.
Denim is a sturdy cotton warp-faced twill textile in which the weft passes
under two or more warp threads.This will weaving produces the familiar diagonal
ribbing of the denim that distinguishes it from cotton duck.
